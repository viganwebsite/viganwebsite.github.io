Vigan Longganisa

Vigan Longganisa is a type of de recado longganisa, which contains ground pork, spices and garlic that is stuffed in an intestine wrapper. which has been influenced by both Western and Eastern cultures since the Colonial Era. It is now a crucial component of the distinctive Vigan cultural heritage.

Vigan Empanada

Grated green papaya or cabbage, entire eggs, and skinless Vigan longganisa make up the delectable Vigan empanada's vegetable filling, which is then wrapped in rice flour or galapong. This iconic Vigan street food tastes excellent when paired with "lasona" (Onion) sukang iloko.

Vigan Bagnet

This popular Ilocano dish is the equivalent of lechon kawali. It is often characterized as large pieces of pig leimpo, belly, or sides that have been twice or more deep-fried till crispy. The ideal accompaniments include tomatoes, onions, and bagoong.

Okilas Ken Dinardaaran

This fantastic duo of dinuguan (pork blood stew) and okilas (chicharon) is perfect for snacking or you can even serve it with rice.The pig skin is turned into chicharon by hanging it for 3-4 days.

Vigan Sinanglao

A dish that is nearly identical to papaitan, sinanglao or sinanglaw is a stew made of bits of beef, bile, and internal organs. a local delicacy that is typically consumed in the morning by residents.

Vigan Miki Noodle Soup

A popular street food in Vigan, this soup is made up of chicken broth, atsuete and most importantly, miki noodles. It is also popularly served after a prayer occasion during Holy Week which is known as Padasal.

Vigan Ukoy/Okoy

A delicious appetizer, Vigan okoy is a crispy shrimp fritter made up of small shrimps and galapong (glutinous rice batter) served along side with the Vigan Empanada. It is commonly meade up of unpeeled shrimps.

Royal Bibingka

Royal bibingka is a soft and cheesy glutinous rice snack that is usually present during holidays,

Turones de Mani

Also known as Peanut Lumpia, it is a little nougat-like confection created in Vigan from honey, egg white, and crushed peanuts that is wrapped in a egg crepe wapper.
