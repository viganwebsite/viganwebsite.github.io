The Citizen's Charter

KATULAGAN: The Vigan Service Guide Book

Vigan City has a lot to offer as the only UNESCO World Heritage City in the Philippines and one of the New Seven Wonders Cities of the World. The City Government of Vigan issued its KATULAGAN: The Vigan Service Guide Book as part of the 250th Anniversary Celebration of Ciudad Fernandina and the Transfer of the Seat of Nueva Segovia.

This book has been thoughtfully developed to support the delivery of services to City people and tourists with stated standards, quality, and time frames, as well as the dedication of officials and staff. Furthermore, the Service Guide Book clarifies, simplifies, and makes public service more accessible to its clients, while emphasizing accountability for its workers.

This guide includes descriptions of local government services, a step-by-step guidance on how to use each service, directions to the relevant office, the requirements, costs, the normal response time for its delivery, and the people in charge of the duties. It also includes options for complaints, compliments, and ideas. Most importantly, it emphasizes the administration's dedication to carrying out its duties and obligations to the people it has pledged to serve.

It is a tool for reducing RED TAPE as well as an enabler for City Government Personnel to efficiently meet the needs of its clients.

Katulagan exemplifies LGU-Vigan's dedication to Good Local Governance.
